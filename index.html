Embarking on the Journey: November 2, 2017
Our vision and mission are to guide young generations, starting from junior high school (SMP), senior high school (SMA/SMK), and their equivalents, who are already "addicted" to gadgets, to become "armed" as skilled app developers.
We also strive to eradicate the "poison" that has been plaguing and spreading relentlessly like "spam" among the youth, leaving them seemingly lost without direction and purpose in life.
